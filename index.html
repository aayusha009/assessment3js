RECIPE GALLERY

Laphing (Tibetan Snack)

Ingredients:
  • 1 cup mung bean starch
  • 4 cups water
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • Salt to taste

Chatpate (Puffed Rice Snack)

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups puffed rice
  • ½ cup boiled potatoes (diced)
  • ½ cup chopped onions
  • ½ cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1 pack Wai Wai noodles (crushed)
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp mustard oil
  • Salt & spices to taste

Chicken Sadeko

Ingredients:
  • 2 cups boiled and shredded chicken
  • ½ cup chopped onions
  • ½ cup chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp mustard oil
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
